How does synchronization work?

Synchronization is performed by requesting various data from various resources.

LSI Launcher

Main application

Most of the functionality is hidden and will be available a little later. Users of the application can already use the hidden functions

Basically, LSI Launcher:

  • Convenient management of the computer and its properties. Can display the necessary indicators on the device information
  • Can be used as an additional security system for monitoring and controlling your computer
  • It has a large list of cool tracks that many will like, you can also suggest adding your track and within 24 hours the new track will appear in the category. (You can include any tracks even if they are not officially in the application)
  • It is impossible to log into your account if you have a two-step authentication installed. It also has NWID protection

System requirements for application

Can work on any computer based on 64-bit and supported OS

Device resource usage (All metrics were checked in real time)

  • Uses 0% of the processor for any actions except listening to tracks. Music uses a little processor often these figures are no more than 0.6% (This number can increase during initializations or processing)
  • Uses 0% of the video card/video adapter, taking into account the operation of the interface at maximum actions
  • uses 69.9 MB of RAM, taking into account the load. When listening to tracks, the indicators increase by a maximum of 70.7 MB. (May vary)
